# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT

**Between:** [Customer Legal Name] ("Customer")  
**And:** Weybre AI ("Processor")  
**Effective Date:** [Date]

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Personal Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person processed by Processor on behalf of Customer.

1.2 **"Processing"** means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.

1.3 **"Data Protection Laws"** means all applicable laws and regulations relating to privacy and data protection, including GDPR and CCPA.

1.4 **"Services"** means the Weybre AI platform and related services provided to Customer.

## 2. SCOPE AND ROLES

2.1 This Agreement applies to all Personal Data processed by Processor in connection with the Services.

2.2 Customer acts as the Data Controller and Processor acts as the Data Processor.

2.3 Processor shall process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Customer, including with regard to transfers of Personal Data to third countries.

## 3. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS

3.1 **Lawful Processing:** Processor shall process Personal Data only in accordance with Customer's documented instructions and applicable Data Protection Laws.

3.2 **Confidentiality:** Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data have committed themselves to confidentiality.

3.3 **Security Measures:** Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:
- Encryption of Personal Data at rest and in transit
- Measures to ensure ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience
- Regular testing and evaluation of security measures
- Incident response procedures

3.4 **Sub-Processors:** Processor may engage sub-processors only with prior written consent from Customer. Current sub-processors are listed in Annex A.

3.5 **Data Subject Rights:** Processor shall assist Customer in responding to requests from data subjects exercising their rights under Data Protection Laws.

3.6 **Data Breach Notification:** Processor shall notify Customer without undue delay upon becoming aware of a Personal Data breach.

3.7 **Deletion:** Upon termination of Services, Processor shall delete or return all Personal Data to Customer, unless required by law to retain it.

## 4. CUSTOMER OBLIGATIONS

4.1 Customer warrants that it has all necessary rights and consents to provide Personal Data to Processor for processing.

4.2 Customer shall ensure that its instructions to Processor comply with Data Protection Laws.

4.3 Customer is responsible for responding to data subject requests, except where Processor is required to respond under applicable law.

## 5. AUDITS AND COMPLIANCE

5.1 Processor shall make available to Customer all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this Agreement.

5.2 Processor shall allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by Customer or an auditor mandated by Customer, subject to reasonable notice and confidentiality obligations.

5.3 Processor maintains documentation of its security measures and compliance with Data Protection Laws.

## 6. DATA TRANSFERS

6.1 Processor may transfer Personal Data outside the European Economic Area only if:
- The transfer is to a country with an adequacy decision, or
- Appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses), or
- Customer has provided explicit consent

6.2 Standard Contractual Clauses are incorporated by reference where applicable.

## 7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

7.1 Each party's liability under this Agreement shall be subject to the limitations set forth in the main Services Agreement.

7.2 Processor shall indemnify Customer for any fines or penalties imposed on Customer due to Processor's breach of this Agreement or Data Protection Laws.

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect for as long as Processor processes Personal Data on behalf of Customer.

8.2 Upon termination, Processor shall delete or return all Personal Data as instructed by Customer.

## 9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws specified in the main Services Agreement.

9.2 Any amendments to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.

9.3 If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.
